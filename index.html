C.A.P.

Citizen’s for Animal Protection

(C.A.P) Foundation is a registered not-for-profit organization (MAH/319/2020) based in Thane, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India with an aim to work towards expanding the circle of compassion towards animals. We run Thane city’s first animal shelter cum educational center which gives shelter to all kinds of animals and promotes peaceful coexistence among all kinds of species including humans.
